GET TO KNOW DR.KIM

 

Dr. Kim’s extensive and unique training background equipped
him to provide his patients the most comprehensive care possible.

 

He graduated with honors from University of Southern California
Ostrow School of Dentistry and was honored to be appointed to
Clinical Assistant Professor right after the graduation at USC.

 

Dr. Kim’s teaching experience at the Special Patient Clinic at USC
in a vigorous environment including prosthodontic treatment for the
mentally handicapped and physically compromised and the aged have
broadened as well as deepened his heart, knowledge and expertise in dentistry.

 

Dr. Kim dedicates to render his patients high quality and most
conservative dental care utilizing the best quality materials and
latest technologies available as he cares for not only patient’s
oral health but overall quality of life.
